Watering

Water lilies should be watered every 2–3 days, or when the top inch of soil feels dry. The best time to water them is in the morning or evening when the sun isn't as strong.

Watering

Full Sun

SUNLIGHT

Most water lilies typically need a minimum of six hours of direct sunlight in order to produce their spectacular blooms.

Sunlight

Indoor

YES

Yes, water lilies can be grown indoors in containers, such as buckets, fountains, or shallow, wide pots. However, they are finicky about indoor conditions and require a lot of light and water temperature control.

Indoor

Zones 3 - 11

HARDINESS

Water lilies can grow in hardiness zones 3–11, and there are two main types: hardy and tropical.

Hardiness
