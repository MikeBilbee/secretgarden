Cosmo flowers are sun worshippers! For optimal growth and vibrant blooms, cosmos thrive in full sun, receiving at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight daily.

While cosmos prefer the abundant sunlight and warmth outdoors, you can start seeds indoors under grow lights for a head start, but transplanting them outside for full sun is necessary for healthy flowering.
