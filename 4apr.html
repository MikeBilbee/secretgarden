Most daisy varieties flower in spring or summer, and the rest of the year they might not have blooms but will still have foliage. So, while they have a blooming season, they are perennials and will come back year after year.

WATERING

Water daisies when the top inch of soil dries out, aiming for 1-2 inches of water per week during their growing season. Adjust watering based on climate and plant stage, but avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.

Watering

SUNLIGHT

Daisies are considered full sun flowers, thriving on at least 6 hours of direct sunlight each day. However, some varieties can tolerate partial shade, especially in hotter climates where intense afternoon sun might scorch the blooms.

Sunlight
